[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the fire when your fingers trace my skin
A slow surrender to the mood that we are in
The midnight shadows dance across the wooden floor
I know my heart has never wanted something more

[Verse 1]
We leave the headlights fading softly in the dust
And trade the motion for a quiet kind of trust
Your breath is falling like a secret on my face
A sweet collision in this hidden little place

[Pre-Chorus]
Every look you give me pulls me closer to the edge
Every whispered promise is a softly spoken pledge
I trace the silver lining painted in your eyes
Beneath the canvas of the starry southern skies

[Chorus]
You are the beautiful destiny I want to hold
A winding river of a story yet untold
The sweetest fever that is running through my veins
A burning fire that a heavy storm sustains

[Post-Chorus]
[Echo] (Oh, beautiful destiny)
The only rhythm that is holding on to me

[Verse 2]

I keep repeating all the softly spoken words you said
The morning light is still a million miles away
And in your gravity is where I want to stay
